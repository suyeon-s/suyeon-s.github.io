Typography

About This Project

I enrolled in the Typography course at UCLA because typefaces significantly impact the user experience in the digital environment. Through this course, I aimed to gain knowledge about the influence of typefaces and apply it to real projects.

I learned about the impact and feelings that typefaces convey to people. Using this knowledge, I designed 40 album covers with Neue Kabel font for my favorite song, Starlight, by Taeyeon. I thought the font could effectively convey an emotive and romantic atmosphere of the song. Furthermore, I created a songbook with 20 of them. I used Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop for this book.
